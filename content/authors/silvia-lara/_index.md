Silvia Lara is the global data lead for gender at LinkedIn’s Economic Graph Research Institute, where she advances understanding of bias and inequality in the workplace through LinkedIn data and insights. Drawing on experience in data consulting, she collaborates with policymakers, editors, and journalists to address meaningful research questions and executes large-scale analytical projects to ensure accuracy, scalability, and rigor. Silvia obtained a bachelor’s degree in computer science from Yale-NUS College.
