name: Alejandra Barrientos
---

Alejandra Barrientos is an economist and consultant in the Development Effectiveness Division (DVF) at IDB Invest, where she works on impact evaluations with a focus on gender and financial inclusion. Before joining IDB Invest, she gained experience in applied research and public policy at the University of Zurich and in Peru’s public sector, including the Ministry of Economy and Finance and the Ministry of Production. She holds a master’s degree in development economics from the University of Oxford.
