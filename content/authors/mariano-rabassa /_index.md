---
name: Mariano Rabassa
---
Mariano Rabassa specializes in the design and evaluation of environmental policies. He holds a Ph.D. In Agricultural and Resource Economics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Currently, he is a Professor at the Department of Economics at Universidad Cat—lica Argentina. Prior to his academic role, he worked as a consultant on climate change-related topics at the World Bank in Washington, DC.
