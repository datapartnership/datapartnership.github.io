Casey Weston leads LinkedIn’s Data for Impact program, which shares aggregated data and insights with governments and leaders to inform public policy, program design, and research. He previously worked at the intersection of data and policy with the New York City Department of Health, the World Bank, and leading U.S. and international financial institutions. He holds a master’s degree in urban planning from New York University and a bachelor’s from Stanford University.
