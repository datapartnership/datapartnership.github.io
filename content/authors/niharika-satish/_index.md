Niharika Satish is a Research Analyst at the EBRD Office of the Chief Economist.

Her research is centred on environmental and development economics in LMICs, leveraging spatial and big data in combination with traditional methods. Before joining EBRD, Niharika completed a Master’s in Economics for Development at the University of Oxford. She also worked as a Research Consultant for the Institute for Financial Management and Research in India.
