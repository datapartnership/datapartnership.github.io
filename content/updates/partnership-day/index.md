**Join us for Development Data Partnership Day on Wednesday, June 3, 2026, from 8:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. (US ET) at the Preston Auditorium, located at the World Bank Group Headquarters in Washington, DC. Additionally, on Friday, June 5, we will host a series of training sessions. During these sessions, members of the Development Data Partnership will learn how to effectively use and apply the data available through the Partnership.**
